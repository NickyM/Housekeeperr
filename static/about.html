Housekeeperr

Self-hosted media library cleanup. Scans your Radarr and Sonarr, checks streaming availability via TMDB, tracks watch state on Plex and/or Jellyfin, and tags items with who requested them via Seerr — so you can free disk on what you've already finished watching.
